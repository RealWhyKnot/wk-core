# wk-core

Shared Editor-only utilities for the WhyKnot VRChat tools. Source of truth for the helpers bundled into [vrc-avatar-qol](https://github.com/RealWhyKnot/vrc-avatar-qol) and [vrcfury-qol](https://github.com/RealWhyKnot/vrcfury-qol) via `scripts/sync-to-downstream.ps1`. Each consumer carries its own copy under `Editor/Internal/`; the standalone VPM package is optional and primarily used by users who want the log-viewer / settings-provider UIs.
